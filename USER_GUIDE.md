# 🎬 MCS Film Club - User Guide

**Quick Start Guide for New Members**

---

## Getting Started (5 minutes)

### 1. Sign Up
1. Go to the site and click **Enter**
2. Click **Sign up** 
3. Enter your:
   - Display name (your real name)
   - Email address
   - Password (at least 6 characters)
4. Click **Create Account**
5. Check your email for a confirmation link
6. Click the confirmation link to activate your account

### 2. Complete Your Profile
After signing up, you'll see your dashboard. Follow these steps:

1. Click **Edit Profile** (or the profile completeness card)
2. **Upload a headshot**:
   - Click the headshot area or drag & drop an image
   - Best: Square photo, clear face, professional or creative
3. **Upload a banner** (optional):
   - Click the banner area or drag & drop
   - Wide landscape image works best
4. Fill in your details:
   - **Role**: Director, Cinematographer, Editor, Actor, etc.
   - **Bio**: Short description about yourself (2-3 sentences)
   - **Website** (optional): Your portfolio site or social media
5. Click **Save Profile**

---

## Adding Your Work

### Portfolio (Your Personal Showcase)

1. From dashboard, click **Next: Add Your Work** or go to **Portfolio**
2. **Upload images**:
   - Click **Upload Images** or drag & drop multiple files
   - Supports: JPG, PNG, GIF
3. **Add video links**:
   - Click **Add Video**
   - Paste YouTube or Vimeo URL
4. For each item:
   - Add a **title** (e.g., "Short Film - The Journey")
   - Add a **description** (what it is, your role)
   - Select a **category** (Film, Photography, Design, etc.)
5. Click **Save All**

### Shared Gallery (NEW! 🎉)

Upload to the collective gallery that everyone can see:

1. Go to **Gallery** in the top menu
2. Click **Upload to Gallery**
3. Drag & drop images or click to upload
4. Add title and description for each image
5. Click **Save All**

**Note**: 
- Everyone can upload to the gallery
- Admins can feature items and edit all uploads
- Your uploads show your name

---

## Customizing Your Page

### Page Builder (Advanced Layout)

1. From dashboard, click **Customize Layout** or go to **Sections**
2. **Create custom sections**:
   - Click a preset (About Me, Featured Work, etc.)
   - Or create custom section with your own title
3. **Add work to sections**:
   - Click **Add Work** to upload new images
   - Drag existing portfolio items into sections
4. **Reorder**:
   - Drag sections up/down to reorder
   - Drag items within sections
5. Click **Save All**

---

## Exporting for Gallery Book (NEW! 📖)

Create a print-ready version of your profile:

1. Go to your public profile:
   - From dashboard, click **View Public Profile**
   - Or go to **Members** and find yourself
2. Click the **Export** button next to your name
3. Review the print layout
4. Click **Download PDF** (top right)
5. Your browser will open the print dialog
6. Save as PDF or print directly

**Tips for best results**:
- Use high-quality images (at least 1200px wide)
- Add titles and descriptions to your work
- Keep bio concise (2-3 sentences)

---

## Viewing Others' Work

### Members Directory
1. Click **Members** in the top menu
2. Browse all visible member profiles
3. Click any profile to see their full page

### Shared Gallery
1. Click **Gallery** in the top menu
2. View all uploaded images from the collective
3. Hover over images to see titles and who uploaded them

---

## Quick Tips

### Profile Visibility
- Your profile is **visible by default** (appears in Members directory)
- To hide your profile: Go to Profile settings → toggle visibility
- Hidden profiles are only visible to you and admins

### Image Guidelines
- **Headshot**: Square, 500x500px minimum, clear face
- **Banner**: Wide, 1920x600px recommended
- **Portfolio**: High quality, 1200px+ width recommended
- **File size**: Keep under 5MB per image for fast loading

### Mobile Use
- All features work on mobile
- Drag & drop works on mobile browsers
- Upload from camera roll on phones

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I edit my profile after saving?**  
A: Yes! Go to Dashboard → Edit Profile anytime

**Q: How do I delete a portfolio item?**  
A: Go to Portfolio or Sections, click the trash icon on the item

**Q: Who can see my profile?**  
A: If visible, everyone can see it. If hidden, only you and admins.

**Q: Can I upload videos?**  
A: Yes, but only via YouTube/Vimeo embed links (not direct uploads)

**Q: How do I change my password?**  
A: Currently handled through email. Contact an admin for password reset.

**Q: What if I can't log in?**  
A: Make sure you confirmed your email. Check spam folder for confirmation link.

## For Admins Only

### Admin Panel Access
- Dashboard → **Admin** button (top right)
- Manage members, toggle visibility, assign roles

### Gallery Management
- Go to Dashboard → Gallery
- You can edit/delete any gallery item
- Click the star icon to feature items

### Member Management
- Admin Panel → Members tab
- Toggle member visibility
- View all profiles (even hidden ones)
